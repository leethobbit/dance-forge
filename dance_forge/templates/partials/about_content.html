About Dance Forge

A platform for dance videos, tutorials, and performances.

Dance Forge was created to provide dancers with a dedicated space to share and discover dance videos. Whether you're a professional dancer, instructor, or dance enthusiast, you can use Dance Forge to:

  • Upload and share your dance videos
  • Create playlists of your favorite routines
  • Track your watch history
  • Connect with other dancers

Our mission is to foster a community where dancers can grow, learn, and be inspired.
